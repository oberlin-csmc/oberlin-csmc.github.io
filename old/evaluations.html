CSMC Course Evaluations


Welcome to the Computer Science Majors' Committee course evaluation pages. Why are we providing an alternative to the official course evaluations? Simple. We believe that students would benefit from reading course evaluations from previous years when deciding whether to take a course in the Computer Science Program. Unfortunately, the professors in the Computer Science Program are unwilling to make the official evaluations public, so we have chosen to do our own evaluations. Please evaluate your courses here in addition to completing the official evaluations.

To fill out course evaluations, please use this form.
