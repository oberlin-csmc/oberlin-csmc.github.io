Reasons for Visiting Grad Schools that Accept you

Hopefully by the time you're thinking about visiting a school, you've already discovered that there are people there doing research which interests you. One important aspect of visiting a school is meeting those people. They are all potential dissertation advisors, and it's a good idea to find out if you can get along with them. Try to make sure that the people who interest you most will be around so you can talk with them when you visit.

Even if you don't get the chance to meet potential advisors, you will learn alot by visiting a school. It's just not possible to get a feel for a place from a brochure or a web page. And there's many questions which just can't be answered remotely.
