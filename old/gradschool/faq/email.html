Update - 15th October 1994

Here is the list of graduate offices email addresses, now I do not guarantee all the addresses as I cannot really check them all, but if you do try some, and you get errors, PLEASE INFORM ME (don't post them on rn), and also if you come across anymore that are not on this list, please forward those to me too.

For those who are not sure about what this all the following addresses are, it is a list of email addresses with which to contact people/offices who can give information about (under) graduate programmes in that department at that university.

To obtain information from these addresses always include your postal address as well, as often they automatically send out a package. Always phrase your request well, remember the person who you write too might decide your future entry. And always be clear.

Many, many thanks to all those who helped in sending me the info, including pointing out the numerous errors that creep in.

And don't forget if you get hold of any addresses please send them to me, so this list will then become even more comprehensive.
