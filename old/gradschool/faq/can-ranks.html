Canadian school rankings

The Rankings shown below have been obtained from the Candian Weekly Magazine "MACLEAN'S", 15 November 1993, pp 28-78. The new statistics, when available, will be promptly included. Please observe however no rating method can be accurate or near accurate. However, these lists do give people a general idea where a particular school stands. If you have a newer list, please e-mail me a copy.

The factors involved in the rankings shown below were :

  • Student body
    1. Average entering grades
    2. Proportion with 75% or higher
    3. Proportion who graduate
    4. Out of Province (1st year)
    5. International Students (Graduate)
    6. Student Awards
  • Classes
    1. Medium Class Size (1st year)
    2. Class Sizes : 1st & 2nd year level
    3. Class Sizes : 3rd & 4th year level
    4. Classes taught by tenured faculty
  • Faculty
    1. Faculty with PhD
    2. Awards for full-time faculty
    3. Humanities Grants
    4. Medical/Science Grants
  • Finances
    1. Operating Budget
    2. Scholarships & Bursaries
    3. Student Services
  • Library
    1. Holdings per student
    2. Acquisitions
    3. Expenses
  • Reputation
    1. Alumini Support
    2. Reputational Survey
