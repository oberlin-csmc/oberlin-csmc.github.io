Introduction to soc.college.grad/gradinfo

Both soc.college.grad and soc.college.gradinfo are unmoderated newsgroups (hereafter called .grad and .gradinfo respectively). The original intent was to make .grad a place for grad-related discussions, and .gradinfo a place to post brochure-type articles. However, this distinction has not been observed in the past; many people crossposted articles to both groups. It has been suggested that people add the word "INFO:" to their subject lines when they are posting informational articles in .grad or University brochures.

Articles submitted to the groups should follow USENET unofficial guidelines (i.e. thou shalt not write more than 70 characters per line, thou shalt trim off as much quoted messages as possible, etc.) Please refer to news.newusers.questions and news.answers.
