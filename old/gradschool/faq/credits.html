Intro

This is an introductory article to the newsgroups soc.college.grad and soc.college.gradinfo. It will be posted every 15 days (around 1st and 15th of each month). Alternatively, a copy may be ftp'd from rtfm.mit.edu:/pub/usenet. For those who do not have ftp, please send a message to "mail-server@rtfm.mit.edu" with "help" in the body for instructions on file retrival.
