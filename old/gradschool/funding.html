Getting Funding

Everyone needs money.
  • Check out Kathi Fisler's Funding Guide for information on how to get some.
  • Mark Krankowitz, from CMU, also has advice on Funding for Graduate School. His www page on funding is amazing. Defenitely check out the FastWEB database (a free service which, given your profile, returns a list of applicable scholarships & fellowships & grants).
  • You might also want to visit the ACM Graduate Assistantship Directory (A directory of school-specific assistantships available to accepted students).
  • GrantWeb is a comprehensive list of sources for grants and other funding opportunities.
  • Rice's list of grants and other funding opportunities.
