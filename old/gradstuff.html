  • Where to find information about specific schools or Areas of CS This page also has some suggestions on how to choose a specific school or area of CS to study in/at.
  • Before you get started on applications, you should have settled on somewhere between 3 and 7 schools to apply to (about). At least one of these schools should be a sure thing, and all of them should be schools you think you can be happy at for 6 years of intensive study and research.
