CSMC Course Evaluation Form

The Computer Science Majors' Committee has set up this form to allow students to communicate with each other about the quality of courses in the computer science program. Unlike the official course evaluations, which provide a forum for feedback to the professor about his or her course, these evaluations are for the benefit of fellow students considering taking a particular course in the department. Please address your evaluation accordingly.

Here are some suggested topics and questions to consider when writing your evaluation.

The CSMC reserves the right to remove any evaluations which do not contribute to this purpose. Evaluations will not be edited.
